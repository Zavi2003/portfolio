Global Station Food Festival

Hands-on entrepreneurship experience through food service and event management

Date July 29, 2025
Role Food Preparation Specialist
Team Size 12 Members

Project Overview

As part of the Professional Skills course, our 12-member team conceptualized, planned, and executed the Global Station food stall for the university food festival. The project combined entrepreneurship, cultural exchange, and event management into a hands-on learning experience.

My Contributions

Prepared and served Lime Mojito, ensuring consistent quality and presentation
Assisted in morning food preparation and stall setup
Collaborated with team members to manage high customer demand during peak hours
Supported inventory management and customer service as needed

Key Achievements

Customer Service Excellence

Successfully served a high volume of customers despite space and equipment constraints

Financial Success

Exceeded revenue expectations through effective pricing and sales strategies

Quality Recognition

Received positive feedback on food quality and team coordination

Skill Development

Developed practical skills in budgeting, food safety, and time management

Challenges & Learning Outcomes

Challenge

High demand for certain items led to early stockouts

Solution

Improved inventory forecasting and backup planning for future events

Challenge

Limited workspace and single cooking equipment created bottlenecks

Solution

Optimized workflow and assigned flexible roles for peak periods

Key Lesson

Real-world experience in adaptive problem-solving, teamwork, and customer engagement

Reflection

This project provided invaluable insights into small-scale entrepreneurship and event execution. I gained confidence in food preparation, teamwork under pressure, and the end-to-end process of running a food stall. The experience highlighted the importance of preparation, communication, and resilience—skills I will carry forward into future professional endeavors.
